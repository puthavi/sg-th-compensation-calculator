Singapore Employment

Thailand — Salary-Heavy (B1)

Assumptions & Inputs

SG: Tax resident (≥183 days). EP holder — no CPF, no FWL. Earned Income Relief 1,000 SGD (under 55). SDL 0.25% of first $4,500.

TH (B1): Salary-heavy — maximize salary, minimal company profit. SME CIT (0% on first 300k THB profit).

TH (B2): Balanced — moderate salary (50k THB/mo) + dividends from company profits. CIT 15% on 300k–3M THB. Dividend WHT 10% (final).

Deductions: Employment income deduction 100k THB (cap), personal allowance 60k THB, SS 9k THB/yr. Single, no dependents.

Sources: PwC SG, PwC TH (PIT), PwC TH (CIT), IRAS
