Singapore Employment

Thailand — Salary-Heavy (B1)

Full salary — all invoice revenue is paid as salary. The company covers employer social security and admin as overhead. No profit distribution or dividends.

Assumptions & Inputs

SG: Tax resident (≥183 days). EP holder — no CPF, no FWL. Earned Income Relief 1,000 SGD (under 55).

TH (B1): Full salary — all invoice revenue paid as salary. Company absorbs employer SS + admin as overhead.

TH (B2): Balanced — moderate salary (50k THB/mo) + dividends from company profits. CIT 15% on 300k–3M THB. Dividend WHT 10% (final).

Deductions: Employment income deduction 100k THB (cap), personal allowance 60k THB, SS 10.5k THB/yr (875/mo, 2026 cap at 17,500 THB/mo wage ceiling). Single, no dependents.

SG Tax Rebate: YA 2026 — 60% of tax payable, capped at SGD 200 (IRAS).

TH Social Security: 5% employee + 5% employer, wage cap 17,500 THB/mo from Jan 2026 (Royal Gazette B.E. 2568 Phase 1).

Sources: PwC SG, PwC TH (PIT), PwC TH (CIT), IRAS
