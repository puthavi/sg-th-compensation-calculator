Important Notes

  • Singapore: Tax resident rates (≥183 days). EP holder — no CPF. Earned Income Relief of 1,000 SGD (under 55).
  • Thailand: Salary-heavy model (maximise salary, minimal company profit). SME CIT rates apply (0% on first 300k THB profit).
  • Thai personal deductions: 100k employment deduction (cap) + 60k personal allowance + 9k social security. Single, no dependents assumed.
  • Social security capped at 750 THB/month per party. Admin costs are estimates. VAT not modeled (revenue likely below 1.8M THB threshold).
  • Exchange rate fluctuations, dividend formalities, and transfer pricing rules should be reviewed with a qualified accountant.
  • Tax rates sourced from PwC Tax Summaries (SG & TH) and IRAS.
